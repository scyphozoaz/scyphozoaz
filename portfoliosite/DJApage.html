My main alias for original works in whatever genres I feel like making. Influenced by metal, techno, and HexD. Click on Archfiend on the right to visit the Bandcamp page.

This alias began mostly as a joke; The first DJ ARCHFIEND album was essentially just a collection of OST music sped up and bitcrushed. Since then, the alias became a stage for me to explore different styles while honing my production, all motivated by producing live sets for the annual Skylive event. It's essentially the audiovisual equivalent of me playing with action figures.

This has culminated in the project's current defining work, 'DJ ARCHFIEND @ SKYLIVE 2024' (shown below), which I feel best displays the current identity and future direction for the alias. If you don't have time to watch the full set, some individual music video clips are showcased.
